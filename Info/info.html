About ClearEntry

ClearEntry is a next-generation trading journal designed to help traders understand the psychology behind their trades.
Track your emotions, analyze patterns, and evolve your strategy with AI-powered insights.

Free Features

• Unlimited trade logging
• Unlimited journal entries
• Basic statistics and analytics
• Trade history and entries viewing

Pro Features

• Full AI-powered analysis
• Complete statistics and analytics
• Trading plan generation
• Calendar view with trade mapping
• Advanced time range analysis
